Second slice of source-MD ingestion pathway (sibling to 023-F). Adds DocFx container parser, [!INCLUDE] resolver, cross-doc link rewriter, and TOC.yml-based ingest ordering on top of the frontmatter strip shipped in 025-S. Together these slices give docline the ability to ingest a Microsoft Learn / DocFx / MkDocs corpus with full structural fidelity (preserved alt-text on images, expanded include content, link rewriting for graph edges, deterministic ingest order). Empirical baseline: Power BI docs (1340 files, surveyed in scripts/study/survey_powerbi_source_md.py). Decision: docs/decisions/2026-06-09-powerbi-source-md-gap-analysis.md and 2026-06-08-source-md-ingestion-extension.md.
