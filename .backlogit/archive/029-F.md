Per operator directive (2026-06-13): the 2026-06-12 empirical study (docs/closure/029-S-adi-spike.md) found ADI loses on every structural fidelity metric vs docling. Strip ADI out entirely and evaluate Mistral OCR as its replacement cloud peer, accessed via Microsoft Foundry MaaS (operator has existing Foundry quota; Foundry is the chosen access path, not Mistral's direct API).

Mistral OCR (mistral-ocr-2503 March 2025, mistral-document-ai-2505 May 2025) is Mistral's dedicated document-to-markdown product. Different design intent from ADI's form-extraction lineage. Reported strengths are tables, equations, code, dense text — precisely the failure modes ADI exhibited.

The official mistralai>=1,<2 SDK works against both Foundry MaaS (custom server_url) and Mistral's direct API. Reader prefers Foundry credentials when AZURE_AI_FOUNDRY_ENDPOINT + AZURE_AI_FOUNDRY_KEY are set; falls back to MISTRAL_API_KEY for direct Mistral. Default model is mistral-document-ai-2505 (newer); operator overrides via MISTRAL_OCR_MODEL env var or --model flag.

Acceptance: AC1-AC10 per docs/plans/2026-06-13-mistral-ocr-replace-adi-plan.md. Key gates: AC1 enforces complete ADI removal; AC4 enforces credential resolution order (Foundry preferred); AC8 closure doc with empirical verdict (ADOPT / PROMOTE-AS-PEER / ABANDON). Auto-policy stays docling-first; Mistral opt-in pending T4 verdict.

Decomposed into 4 tasks (T1-T4) totaling ~9.5h human-equivalent effort + ~$0.55 study cost. T1 removes ADI; T2 adds Mistral reader (Foundry-first credentials); T3 wires it through full surface; T4 runs empirical study + writes closure doc.

Supersedes the 029-S KEEP-AS-PEER verdict by removing ADI. Consumes part of B26003B0 (forms/invoices re-validation becomes moot if ADI is gone).
