Replace page-based and char-bin segmentation in `src/docline/process/output_contract.py` with a heading-aware semantic segmenter (`src/docline/process/segment.py`) that splits at H1 boundaries (hard), sub-splits at H2 when a part exceeds `max_chars` (default 12_000), and falls through to deterministic char-binning as the safety net when no headings exist or sub-splitting cannot reach the target size. Applies to PDF (after extractor output) and DOCX (after `read_docx_blocks`). Web crawl unchanged. No new dependency (`markdown-it-py` already used by `ast_lint.py` and `heading_validation.py`). No schema break — filenames remain `part-NNNN.md`. Harvested from stash `90695245` (G3a). G3b (frontmatter referentiality + chunk anchors) and G3c (docling + sidecars) are explicitly out of scope and will ship in subsequent cycles.
