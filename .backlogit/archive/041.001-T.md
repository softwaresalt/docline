Compute the per-host ocr_max_pages from resource_probe.probe() + ocr_budget and pass it into group_by_page_count_ocr_aware and dispatch_batched_groups_with_retry at the two call sites (pdf_batch.py ~286, pdf_triage.py ~572). Keep OCR_MAX_BATCHED_PAGES=8 as ceiling / degraded-probe fallback.

<!-- BEGIN:acceptance-criteria -->
- A helper derives ocr_max_pages from probe().available_ram_gb (-> available_mb) and ocr_budget.max_ocr_pages_per_group using a representative page_megapixels from the OCR PDFs' first-page mediabox.
- pdf_batch.py (~286) and pdf_triage.py (~572) pass ocr_max_pages=<derived> to group_by_page_count_ocr_aware and dispatch_batched_groups_with_retry.
- The derived cap is host-relative: a high-RAM host caps HIGHER than 8, a low-RAM host lower (OCR_MAX_BATCHED_PAGES=8 is the degraded-probe FALLBACK, not a ceiling).
- When the probe is unavailable/degraded or no OCR items exist, the cap falls back to OCR_MAX_BATCHED_PAGES (8).
- Tests: derived cap larger on a high-RAM fake budget, smaller on a low-RAM fake budget; degraded probe -> fallback 8.
<!-- END:acceptance-criteria -->
