title: Scale-step retry in batch_dispatch for single-page OCR OOM
updated_at: 2026-06-30T19:10:16.6365027-07:00
---

Wire a scale-step retry into src/docline/process/batch_dispatch.py::dispatch_batched_groups_with_retry so a single-page OCR group that still OOMs after group-downsizing is retried at progressively reduced ocr_scale before conceding to heuristic.

Current behavior (038.003-T): the worklist halves the pages-per-group cap (8->4->2->1); a crashed OCR group with len==1 (or cap==1) is given up (returncode recorded -> caller falls back to heuristic). New behavior: for a crashed OCR group that can no longer be split (len==1 or cap==1), before giving up, re-dispatch that item at a reduced ocr_scale (e.g. step 1.0 -> 0.5 -> 0.25 down to a floor like 0.25), writing the `ocr_scale` field into the manifest chunk (uses the knob from 040.001-T). Only concede to heuristic once the scale floor is reached and still crashes. Keep OCR-free groups and multi-item groups unchanged. Guarantee termination (finite scale steps + finite cap halving).

Design: track a per-worklist-entry ocr_scale (start None = full). When a single-item OCR entry crashes and cap<=1 (or len==1) and ocr_scale can still step down, re-enqueue the same item with the next lower scale. Add an ocr_scale_steps sequence (e.g. (0.5, 0.25)) as a module constant. Manifest chunk includes ocr_scale only when set.

Tests: tests/process/test_batch_dispatch.py — a fake runner that crashes a single OCR item at full/high scale but SUCCEEDS once ocr_scale <= 0.5 (inspect the manifest's ocr_scale); assert the item recovers (rc 0, envelope written) after the downscale retry, and that a runner which crashes at ALL scales terminates and concedes (rc != 0) with bounded attempts. Width: src/docline/process/batch_dispatch.py + tests/process/test_batch_dispatch.py.

<!-- BEGIN:acceptance-criteria -->
- A single-page OCR item that crashes at full scale is retried at reduced ocr_scale (manifest carries ocr_scale) and recovers when the runner succeeds at the lower scale.
- A single item that crashes at every scale terminates (bounded attempts) and concedes to heuristic (rc != 0).
- OCR-free and multi-item behavior unchanged; existing test_batch_dispatch.py + test_bounded_subbatching.py pass.
- Gates green: ruff, pyright src/ (0 errors), pytest, ruff format --check.
