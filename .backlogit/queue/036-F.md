Spike: quantify whether tighter triage coalescing (lower merge_gap) cuts docling wall-clock without fidelity loss. The 2026-06-25 routing investigation found merge_gap=2 absorbed +981 pages (+54% over the 1818 raw-flagged) into docling ranges (82% routed). Now that 037-S bounded sub-batching amortizes docling model-load across a group, smaller/tighter ranges are cheaper, so the cold-start penalty that motivated aggressive merging is reduced. Decomposed into an agent-shippable comparison harness + an operator-run cosmos sweep (heavy docling inference). Verdict artifact decides whether to lower the merge_gap default. Evidence: docs/decisions/2026-06-25-cosmos-docling-routing-investigation.md; the 038-S docling_attribution metric makes the routing measurable accurately.
